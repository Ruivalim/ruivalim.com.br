About Me

Hello! I’m Rui Valim, a passionate Software and DevOps Engineer with almost a decade of experience in programming. My journey has taken me through various industries, including a significant tenure at Toyota, where I honed my skills in cloud computing and backend development. Apart from coding, I love capturing the world through my lens, and I’m excited to share my photography work here.
